Why Donate Blood?

  • The most precious gift that one can give to another person is the gift of life i.e. blood. It is the essence of life.
  • Your blood saves more than one life when it is seperated into its components (red blood cells, plasma etc).
  • Blood is needed regularly for patients with diseases such as thalassemia and hemophilia, and also for the treatment of injuries after an accident, major surgeries, anemia, etc.
  • It imporoves your health.

Facts About Blood

  1. One unit of donated blood can save up to three lives.
  2. You can donate blood every three months. It only takes 48 hours for your body fluid to be completely replenished.
  3. Scientiest have estimated the volume of blood in the human body to be eight percent of body weight.
  4. There are 100000 miles of blood vessels in an adult human body.
